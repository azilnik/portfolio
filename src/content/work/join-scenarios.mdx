Join is a project management platform that helps construction companies, owners, and architects make better, more informed decisions.

Scenarios enable multidisciplinary construction teams to present, price, and decide which ideas will be implemented. This feature also lets owners explore possible project pathways and associated cost implications. It groups and presents varied decision scenarios in a clear, easy-to-understand format.
