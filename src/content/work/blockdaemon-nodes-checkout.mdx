Blockdaemon is a blockchain infrastructure platform, enabling any company to connect to blockchains.

I joined Blockdaemon as the first designer, leading the design their web and mobile apps. These apps allow users to access real-time data across all their different blockchain assets in one place.

Deploying a blockchain node historically has required complex technical and operational knowledge. The new node checkout workflow helps customers deploy servers on their own, and trust Blockdaemon to keep the servers reliable and stable in the face of a rapidly-changing technology landscape.
