Two zero-to-one products shipped in 15 months: institutional staking dashboard and the Ubiquity developer API. No established UX patterns in crypto for either. Joined as founding designer after Blockdaemon acquired my consultancy. Company raised $207M at $3.25B valuation during tenure.

## Institutional staking

Treasury teams managing hundreds of millions across chains. They needed total value and validator performance on one screen. Density plus real-time trust signals.

## Ubiquity API

Developer-facing API for querying crypto data across chains. The dashboard was where developers debugged what their API keys were doing. Highest-value views: usage against rate limits and request-log auditing.

Staking product shipped into $207M Series C materials. The screens investors walked through in demos traced back to my team. Grew the design org from 1 to 7.
